Business Context

The Garment Industry is a vital component of the global industrial landscape, showcasing the impact of industrial globalization in the modern era. Known for its labor-intensive nature and reliance on manual processes, the industry faces the challenge of meeting the substantial global demand for garment products. The efficiency and performance of employees in garment manufacturing companies play a crucial role in satisfying this demand. Decision-makers in the industry have a keen interest in monitoring, analyzing, and predicting the productivity performance of working teams within their factories.
